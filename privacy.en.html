Privacy Policy

The app "Palette Store" (the "App")

The developer of the App (the "Developer", "we") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how information is handled in the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect information that personally identifies you (such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts).

The information handled by the App is as follows:

  • Game scores, progress, and settings: Stored locally on your device. If iCloud is turned on, this data is synced via Apple's iCloud (key-value store) and shared across devices using the same Apple Account.
  • Game Center data: When you use rankings or achievements, this is recorded through Apple's Game Center.

2. Sharing and External Transmission

  • We do not send or store your information on any server operated by us.
  • The App shows no ads. We do not use any third-party advertising networks or analytics (tracking) SDKs.
  • We do not use your data for the purpose of tracking you.

3. Apple Services

The App uses iCloud and Game Center, which are services provided by Apple. Information handled through these services is subject to Apple's privacy policy. For details, please see the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Use by Children

The App is designed so that children can use it safely: it does not collect personal information or show behaviorally targeted advertising. Please use it under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

5. Deleting Your Data

Data stored on your device is erased when you delete the App. Data synced to iCloud can be managed and deleted from your device under Settings > Apple Account > iCloud. Game Center data can be reviewed in the Game Center settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy as needed. If there are significant changes, we will announce them on this page.
